About SubTrack

Manage all your subscriptions in one place

Our Mission

SubTrack was created to simplify how people manage their subscriptions. From entertainment apps like Netflix and Spotify to essential tools like Google Drive or Microsoft 365, users often lose track of renewals and payments.

Our goal is to help users stay organized, save money, and never miss a renewal date.

Why Choose Us?

  • Track all subscriptions in one dashboard
  • Get timely reminders before renewals
  • Monitor monthly and yearly spending
  • Identify unused services and save money

Real-World Example

Imagine you have Netflix, Spotify, and Google Drive subscriptions. Instead of checking each app, SubTrack shows your total monthly cost, notifies you about renewals, and helps you decide whether to continue or cancel a service.
